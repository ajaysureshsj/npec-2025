Important Notes

  • Based on availability, student participants may get hostel accommodation for 4 days (13-12-2025 to 17-12-2025) on charge basis. More details will be provided on the conference website.

  • Student registrations are valid for inclusion of papers in the conference proceedings.
  • Any registration includes access to all conference activities, breakfasts, lunches, dinners, and coffee breaks.
  • Registration fees are non-refundable.
  • An 18% GST will be added to each registration fee. Additional transaction fees may apply.
